MY FAVOURITE BOLLYWOOD ACTORS


AMITABH BACHCHAN


Amitabh Bachchan is an Indian actor, film producer, television host, occasional playback singer and former politician known for his work in Hindi cinema. He is regarded as one of the most influential actors in the history of Indian cinema.He gained popularity for films Zanjeer, Deewaar,Sholay,Don,Coolie,Amar Akbar Anthony;and was dubbed India's "angry young man" for his on-screen roles in Hindi films.

RAJINIKANTH


Rajinikanth is an Indian actor, producer and screenwriter who works predominantly in Tamil cinema, where he is also referred to as Super Star.He became an all-india star for his movies Andha Kanoon,Baasha,Chaandramukhi,Enthiran(Robot),Kaabali,Kaala,2.0 etc.

AKSHAY KUMAR


Rajiv Hari Om Bhatia,known professionally as Akshay Kumar, is an Indian-born naturalised Canadian actor and film producer who works in Hindi cinema.He become popular for his films Hera Pheri,Bhool Bhulaiyaa,Baby,Pad-man,Housefull,Airlift etc.

JOHN ABRAHAM


John Abraham is an Indian actor, film producer, writer and former model working in Hindi films.He has since starred in successful films like Dhoom,Dostana,New York,Housefull 2,Race 2,Parmanu,Satyameva Jayate and Batla House.

SUSHANT SINGH RAJPUT


Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema.He starred in a number of commercially successful Hindi films such as M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story,Kedarnath and Chhichhore.

EMRAAN HASHMI


Emraan Anwar Hashmi is an Indian actor who appears in Hindi cinema.His critically and commercially successful films include Awarapan,Jannat,Raaz: The Mystery Continues,Once Upon a Time in Mumbaai,Shanghai and Jannat 2.
